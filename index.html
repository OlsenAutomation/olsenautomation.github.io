Approach

The work is strongest when AI handles the heavy lift and a human remains responsible for truth, usefulness, and delivery quality.

Operating philosophy

AI is leverage, not autopilot.

Good automation still needs clear intent, accurate inputs, tight constraints, and review. The goal is to ship useful systems and assets without letting speed replace judgment.

  1. 1Define the real job the output needs to do.
  2. 2Separate source truth from noise, damage, or accidental artifacts.
  3. 3Use AI to accelerate exploration, reconstruction, drafting, and production.
  4. 4Review, correct, document, and ship with clear limitations.
